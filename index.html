Together We Have the Power to Build a Better World for Everyone

Raise Chess Academy is a refugee led organization that is transforming the lives of refugee children and youths of different cultures, backgrounds, communities and nationalities through sports, chess and education. For refugee youths who have escaped wars and other challenging situations RCA becomes a rehabilitation place and a bridge for success and transformation of their lives again. Many refugee youths lose hope as they come to new communities where a different language is spoken and face discrimination and negativity, sports and chess is now seen as a tool to combat barriers and restore hope for the negatively affected refugee children and youths.
